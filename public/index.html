Getting started

Three steps to autotweeting:

  1. Login to Lyrebird with your twitter account.
  2. Refine your terms using the built in search.
  3. Press Retweet to enable auto tweeting of future results.

Why use Lyrebird?

  • Retweet mentions of your product or event automatically.
  • Create a twitter bot to capture your favorite searches. Follow to see live results in your stream or share with friends
