Getting started

Three steps to autotweeting:

  1. Login to Lyrebird with your twitter account.
  2. Select search terms to tweet.

    3. Use Lyrebird's built in twitter search to see previous tweets and fine tune your search. Don't worry, auto tweeting is disabled until you press "Retweet".

  3. Press Retweet to save search for auto retweeting.

    4. All future results for the selected search will automatically be retweeted. Lyrebird won't tweet anything that has already been posted. If you decide to stop retweeting simply press "Stop Retweeting".
